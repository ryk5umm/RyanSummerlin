We attend a public meeting
in a big red barn. In the Methow Valley,
the concern is about plans to
bring Grizzly Bears into the neighboring
North Cascades National Park.

Diablo Lake,
North Cascades
National Park

Immediately upon arriving
in Oklahoma we spot
a blue heron perched
in a pasture cow pond.
I’m suprised, because
I was used to seeing them
on the Washington coast.

Hovering nearby, the glowing face
standing as evidence of gravity’s reach.
In Oklahoma skies, the climate
stays uncommonly warm.
I’ve never captured details of the moon
as with this 100-400 mm lens.

At a country road Air BNB,
my wife’s family
came to visit my hometown.
It’s just a few miles
from the house
where I grew up. In the pasture
are a couple miniture donkeys,
at night we hear
coyotes in numbers,
maybe a dozen or more.

Next to Highway 101,
between Olympia and Shelton,
a river empties into
a tide flat of the Puget Sound,
where shorebirds and
bald eagles flock.
